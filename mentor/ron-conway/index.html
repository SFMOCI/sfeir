Ron Conway

Ron Conway is Special Adviser to SV Angel, a San Francisco-based angel investment firm that helps technology startups with business development, financing and strategic advice. He has invested and worked closely with startups since the mid 1990's, including Google and facebook. A native San Franciscan, Conway founded sf.citi in 2012 to engage the technology community with the life of his home city. - See more at: http://www.sfciti.com/board#sthash.zFKs23ZT.dpuf
