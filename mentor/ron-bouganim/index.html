Ron Bouganim

Ron is the Managing Director of the Govtech Fund, the first-ever venture fund focused on startups that transform government. Previously, Ron was a member of the founding management teams of half a dozen startups in the telecommunication, digital media and software sectors, building companies from an idea through to successful exits to public companies including British Telecom and Rovi. Ron has raised over $300 Million in venture capital and vendor financing and been engaged in 7 M&A transactions. Ron has also been actively involved with a number of nonprofits as a donor, adviser and board member including Kiva, Foundation For Defense Of Democracies, Full Circle Fund, Endeavor, Presidio Knolls School and Code For America. As an angel investor and advisor, Ron has worked closely with more than 20 startups over the past 6 years including ShareThrough, HelloSign, PagerDuty, Close.io, and Breakthrough.
